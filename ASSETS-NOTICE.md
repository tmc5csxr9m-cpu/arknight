## Maintainer Rules / 维护规则

- Do not describe game assets as open-source assets.
- Do not add third-party assets unless their status is documented.
- If a rights holder requests removal, remove the affected assets promptly.
- For commercial or redistributable variants, replace all game assets and
  game-derived text with materials you are licensed to use.

- 不要把游戏素材描述为开源素材。
- 不要在未记录授权状态的情况下加入第三方素材。
- 如权利方要求移除，应及时移除受影响素材。
- 如果要制作可商用或可再分发版本，应替换所有游戏素材和游戏衍生文本。
